KeyngFu

Kick Virtual Keyboards KeyngFu Style!

Virtual keyboard hinders accessibility, creates confusion, and most importantly, makes you less secure by forcing you to choose a simple password. Just to get rid of it!

There are lots of websites that try to "secure" their login systems against keyloggers, such as banks, by implementing these keyboards. This is not an ideal solution. Keyloggers now a days are capable of doing much more than logging your keystrokes.

KeyngFu tries to disable those virtual keyboards, in order to:

  • Give you a better security by letting you choose and type stronger passwords
  • Increase your accessibility to the web page, especially for handicapped
  • Eliminate the confusion created by randomizing virtual keyboard's keys
KeyngFu It!

How To Use It?

  1. Drag the “KeyngFu It” button to your Bookmarks bar
  2. When you encounter a protected field with virtual keyboard, click the “KeyngFu It” button

F.A.Q

Does it work on all websites?

KeyngFu has been tested on many websites, and held its ground. If you see any issue with a particular website, feel free to submit an issue.

How do I trust you?

You don't! That's rule number 1 in security. You shouldn't trust anyone but yourself. However, the source code of KeyngFu is publicly open. Which means you can look at the code implementation, or take it to an expert if you don't have the knowledge.
